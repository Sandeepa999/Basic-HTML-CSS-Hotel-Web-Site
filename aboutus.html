    ABOUT US


    A timeless beauty, engraved amidst a colonial landscape formerly known as The Silvas Beach Hotel, pioneering hospitality since 1981.

    We have been categorically rated as the Best of the Best in Banqueting and Culinary excellence having catered to a generation with remarkable memoirs. Our finest gallantry of service personnel was handpicked to preserve the flavor of service par excellence.

    Our Chef De-Cuisine, a veteran in culinary art, has meticulously blended together the choicest ingredients to serve you with a variety of mouth watering menu’s that surely will want you to come back for more.

    Whilst serving you with the best we also have carefully considered the logistics to create the most compatible budgets for your forthcoming gala event.

    Please make your next appointment with us, should it be a wedding, a private function, a corporate event or whatever the nature of business may be, we are equipped to serve your all.

    Our Banquet halls are designed to cater to your multiple choices, If you’re looking to hold a very private function, exclusively behind closed doors, we have the right venue for you cut above the rest, or an open door event, we can begin with the least 100 pax and stretch to 700 pax, its yours for the asking. Our seating capacity, Banquet style, is 700 pax, whilst theatre style can take in 1200 max.

    We have the finest Banquet hall in town, with interiors of colonial elegance, a magnificent custom made chandelier hosting 1200 lights, a blend of other lighting effects and air-conditioned comfort, all of these put together to make your dream come true at The UB Hotel, Colombo.
